Mental Wellness

Your mind matters just as much as your body. Explore techniques to reduce stress, sleep better, and live mindfully.

🧘 Meditation Techniques
  • Box Breathing — inhale 4s, hold 4s, exhale 4s
  • Body Scan — focus attention from head to toe
  • Guided Visualisation — imagine a calm, safe place
  • Mantra Meditation — silently repeat a calming word
  • 5-Minute Morning Sit — just observe your breath
😴 Sleep Habits
  • Maintain a consistent sleep & wake time daily
  • Avoid screens at least 30 minutes before bed
  • Keep your bedroom cool, dark, and quiet
  • Avoid caffeine after 3 PM
  • Try a short bedtime journaling routine
🌬️ Stress Management
  • Identify your stress triggers and write them down
  • Take short breaks every 60–90 minutes of work
  • Practice progressive muscle relaxation
  • Limit news & social media consumption
  • Talk to someone you trust when overwhelmed
☀️ Positive Lifestyle
  • Start each day with one thing you're grateful for
  • Spend at least 20 minutes outdoors daily
  • Set small, achievable goals every morning
  • Celebrate progress, not just perfection
  • Surround yourself with supportive people
🏃 Exercise & Mental Health
  • 30 min of walking reduces anxiety significantly
  • Yoga combines movement with mindful breathing
  • Exercise releases endorphins — natural mood lifters
  • Team sports build social connection & confidence
  • Even stretching for 10 min improves your mood
🍃 Mindfulness Practices
  • Eat one meal a day without screens — mindful eating
  • Do a 5-4-3-2-1 grounding exercise when anxious
  • Take 3 deep breaths before responding in conflict
  • Notice 3 beautiful things on your way to class
  • End the day by writing down one win, however small
