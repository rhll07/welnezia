Training Tips

How many days should I train per week?

Beginners should aim for 3–4 days per week. Advanced athletes can train 5–6 days with proper recovery built in.

Should I do cardio before or after weights?

For fat loss, do cardio after weights. For endurance goals, cardio first is fine. Either way, warm up before both.

How long should a workout session be?

45–60 minutes is ideal for most people. Quality over quantity — short focused sessions beat long unfocused ones.

Is rest important?

Absolutely. Muscles grow during rest, not during training. At least 1–2 rest or active recovery days per week is essential.
